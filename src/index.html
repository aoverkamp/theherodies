About Me

I’m Kevin Warren, a freelance illustrator from St. Louis, Missouri. I am currently living out my dream of being a comic book artist. My comic projects include Copernicus Jones: Robot Detective with writer Matt D. Wilson and co-writing and illustrating OFFERING and SACRIFICE with writer Ken Lowery.
